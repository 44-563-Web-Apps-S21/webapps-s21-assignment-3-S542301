The choclate Uakari

Uakaris are typically lethargic and silent in zoo conditions, but in the wild they are agile and active, capable of leaps of over 6 meters.

  1. They have been observed both in small groups and in larger troops
  2. When traveling through the forest they move in the lower branches of the trees, though when foraging they also go up to the canopy
  3. They eat fruit, nuts, buds and leaves
