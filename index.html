palak panner

  1. Paneer (Indian cottage cheese) is cooked with spinach and spices in this creamy and flavorful curry.
  2. It pairs well with naan, paratha or jeera rice.
  3. Palak Paneer is such a staple in north India. It’s one of the most commonly made paneer dishes in Indian homes.
    1. The spinach is blanched and then pureed. It’s then cooked with spices and simmered on low heat with paneer.
    2. Cream is added to cut down on the bitterness of the spinach leaves and also make it rich.
    3. Everyone has their own way of making this dish and honestly, I also make it in a different way every time I cook it.
